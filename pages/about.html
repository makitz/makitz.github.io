About

We are Tanzanian creatives specialising in designing, creating and disseminating digital and promotional content and managing online communities. Our specialities include digital marketing, visual designs (graphics, animation, photography, e.t.c), web development and more.

Maki is a Tanzanian company - registered with BRELA - founded on August 2022 by two long-time colleagues to further their dream of creating and promoting quality digital content - our dream!

We have worked in various parts of the digital and its supporting industries as employees, consultants, partners, and more, since at least 2015. Maki is our effort to consolidate all our work in one place, where technical and managerial decisions will be executed by the same individuals undertaking the work. With the experience and know-how we bring to the table, we deliver creative, quality, and affordable digital services and products.

Key Philosophy

“Each person who ever was or is or will be has a song. It isn't a song that anybody else wrote. It has its own melody, it has its own words. ― Neil Gaiman, Anansi Boys

Our decisions are influenced by what we love and what we don't. What we love is influenced by what we know. And what we know is influenced by the stories we have collected throughout our lives. We believe that all content, from coding to animation, to graphics, is a way of storytelling. And if you tell it the right way, you are more likely to get more people to listen and respond. This is what we aim to do for brands in the digital space.
